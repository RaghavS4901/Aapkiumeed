Who all are not eligible to donate plasma?

  1. Women who have ever been pregnant.
  2. People vaccinated for Covid-19 less than 28 days ago (Covid-infected persons are ineligible for plasma donation for 28 days since inoculation).
  3. Anyone who does not have enough antibodies present in their blood.

when can you donate plasma?

  1. 14 days have passed after a Covid-19 positive report(if asymptomatic).
  2. 14 days after symptoms have disappeared(if symptomatic).
  3. a person who had recovered from COVID-19 can donate plasma around 30 to 40 days after testing positive as they would have adequate number of antibodies. â€œThose between the ages of 18 and 55 can donate. People with hypertension and diabetes too are eligible if all other conditions are under control.

THINGS TO BE AWARE OF DURING PLASMA DONATION

  1. Contact the hospital prior to your visit and inform them of your plans/ask for any additional information they might have on the matter.
  2. Carry a hard copy of your Covid-19 negative report, whether RT-PCR or a Rapid Antigen test, within 4 months of the donation day
  3. Donate only after 14 days of a COVID-19 positive report if the person is asymptomatic or after 14 days of symptoms if the person is symptomatic.
  4. Women who have ever been pregnant cannot donate COVID-19 convalescent plasma.
  5. A person who has received COVID-19 vaccination will not be able to donate plasma for 28 days from the date of vaccination.
  6. A person can not donate if he/she gets rejected for the lack of adequate antibodies in the blood.

SOME COMMON MYTHS AND THEIR REALITY

  1. If I donate Plasma then, my immunity level will drop
  2. Plasma, the liquid content of your blood that has antibodies is only collected after you have completely recovered. WBC and RBC responsible for your immunity are flushed back into the system
  3. One Donor can save one life
  4. Most of the time, one donor's plasma can save two lives. At times some plasma receivers may need two units(200ml each)
  5. I cannot donate plasma again
  6. Unlike blood, plasma regenerates within four hours of donation. so, one can donate plasma again in 15days.
